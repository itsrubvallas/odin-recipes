Pizza


A pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a round, flatbread base topped with various ingredients, typically including tomato sauce, cheese (often mozzarella), and a variety of toppings such as pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, peppers, olives, and meats like sausage or ham. The pizza is then baked in an oven, resulting in a delicious, savory meal enjoyed worldwide for its versatility and flavor combinations.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dought
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Toppings
  • Olive oil
  • Herbs and spice

Directions

  1. Prepare the dought
    • In a large mixing bowl,combine flour, water, yeast, salt and olive oil.
    • Knead the dough until it's smooth and elastic.
    • Cover the dough with a damp cloth or plastic wrap and let it rise in a warm place until it doubles in size, usually about 1-2 hours.

  2. Preheat the oven
    • Preheat your oven to the highest temperature possible, typically around 475-500°F (245-260°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.

  3. Prepare the toppings
    • While the dough is rising, prepare your toppings. This includes slicing any vegetables, grating cheese, and preparing any meats.

  4. Shape the dought
    • Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release the air.
    • On a lightly floured surface, roll out the dough into a round shape, about 12-14 inches (30-35 cm) in diameter. You can adjust the thickness to your preference.

  5. Assemble the pizza
    • Transfer the rolled-out dough to a lightly greased pizza pan or baking sheet, or onto a preheated pizza stone if using.
    • Spread a layer of tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
    • Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
    • Add your desired toppings evenly over the cheese layer.

  6. Bake the pizza
    • Carefully transfer the assembled pizza into the preheated oven.
    • Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
    • Keep an eye on the pizza while it bakes to prevent burning.

  7. Serve and enjoy
    • Once the pizza is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
    • Slice the pizza into wedges and serve hot. You can drizzle olive oil or sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese and fresh herbs over the top before serving, if desired.
