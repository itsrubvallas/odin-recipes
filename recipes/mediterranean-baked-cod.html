Mediterranean Baked Cod with Lemon

This Mediterranean baked cod with lemon, deliciously seasoned with fresh Mediterranean herbs, garlic, and lemon, is ready in 25 minutes, start to finish. Serve with your favorite potato dish, and a green vegetable or salad, and your meal is done.

Ingredients

  • 4 cod fillets (about 6 ounces each)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lemon, thinly sliced
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven
    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Prepare the Cod:
    • Rinse the cod fillets under cold water and pat them dry with paper towels.
    • Place the cod fillets in a baking dish that has been lightly greased with olive oil.

  3. Season the Cod:
    • In a small bowl, mix together the minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper.
    • Drizzle the olive oil over the cod fillets, then sprinkle the seasoning mixture evenly over the top of each fillet, rubbing it in gently with your fingers.

  4. Add Lemon Slices:
    • Arrange lemon slices over the top of each cod fillet. You can squeeze a little lemon juice over the fish as well if desired.

  5. Bake the Cod:
      6. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for about 15-20 minutes, or until the cod is opaque and flakes easily with a fork.

  6. Garnish and Serve:
    • Once the cod is cooked through, remove it from the oven.
    • Sprinkle chopped fresh parsley over the top of the cod fillets for garnish.
    • Serve the Mediterranean baked cod with lemon hot, accompanied by lemon wedges for extra flavor if desired.
