Chicken Enchiladas Suizas

Chicken enchiladas Suizas is a delicious Mexican dish with creamy sauce and tender chicken.

Ingredients

For the chicken filling:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded (you can use rotisserie chicken)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped.
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced.
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro

For the Suiza Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup salsa verde (green salsa)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Other ingredients

  • 10-12 corn tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded cheese (such as Monterey Jack or a blend of cheeses)
  • Additional chopped cilantro, for garnish
  • Sliced jalapeños (optional, for garnish)

Directions

  1. Prepare the Chicken Filling:
    • In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until softened.
    • Add shredded chicken to the skillet. Season with salt, pepper, ground cumin, and chili powder. Stir to combine.
    • Cook for a few minutes until the chicken is heated through and well-coated with the seasonings.
    • Remove from heat and stir in chopped cilantro. Set aside.

  2. Prepare the Suiza Sauce:
    • In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Add all-purpose flour and whisk until smooth
    • Gradually add chicken broth, whisking constantly to prevent lumps from forming.
    • Stir in sour cream and salsa verde. Season with salt and pepper.
    • Cook the sauce, stirring frequently, until it thickens slightly. Remove from heat and set aside.

  3. Assemble the Enchiladas:
    • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
    • Warm the corn tortillas briefly in the microwave or on a skillet until they are pliable.
    • Place a spoonful of the chicken filling in the center of each tortilla, roll it up, and place it seam-side down in a baking dish.
    • Repeat with the remaining tortillas and filling, arranging the enchiladas snugly in the baking dish.

  4. Pour the Suiza Sauce and Add Cheese:
    • Pour the prepared Suiza sauce evenly over the top of the enchiladas.
    • Sprinkle shredded cheese over the sauce-covered enchiladas.

  5. Bake the Enchiladas:
    • Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes, or until the enchiladas are heated through and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  6. Serve:
    • Remove the enchiladas from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.
    • Garnish with additional chopped cilantro and sliced jalapeños, if desired, before serving.
    • Serve warm and enjoy your delicious chicken enchiladas Suizas!
